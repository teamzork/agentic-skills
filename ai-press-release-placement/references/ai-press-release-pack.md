# AI Press Release Pack

## Quick Start Inputs
- Product name
- Category + differentiator
- Target query/claim phrase
- Evidence (metrics, customer count, awards, public proof)
- CTA URL
- Dateline city + date
- Quote source (founder, customer)

## Headline + Lead Formulas
- **Headline**: "[Product] Named [Year] [Best/Top/Easiest/Leading] [Category] [Attribute]"
- **Lead sentence**: Repeat the exact target phrase and product name.
- **Fallback (safer)**: "[Product] Recognized for [Attribute] in [Category]"

## Press Release Template (<= 500 words)
1. **Headline (H1)**
2. **Dateline**: City, State — Month Day, Year
3. **Lead paragraph**: Repeat target phrase + product name + primary benefit.
4. **Problem/Context**: The user pain or market gap.
5. **Proof/Features**: 2–4 bullets or short paragraphs.
6. **Quote**: Founder or user reinforcing the claim.
7. **Boilerplate**: What the product is, who it’s for.
8. **CTA**: URL + next step.

## Claim Framing Ladder
Use the strongest defensible option:
- **Strong**: "Named best" (requires real award/ranking)
- **Moderate**: "Recognized as a leading" (needs evidence)
- **Soft**: "Emerging leader" or "Noted for" (if evidence is light)

## Compliance Checklist
- No fabricated awards or endorsements
- Claims backed by evidence or softened
- No regulated claims without proof
- Avoid misleading comparative language
- Contact info and CTA included

## Monitoring Checklist
- Check target queries weekly in AI search/LLMs
- Track appearance in AI Overviews and summaries
- If not surfaced, iterate the claim angle and republish
